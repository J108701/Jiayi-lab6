Projects

Tarot Beer

This portfolio entry details my branding design for Tarot Brews, an innovative dark beer brewery that incorporates tarot card aesthetics into its branding. Tasked with designing the logo, posters, and beer bottle packaging, I sought to merge the mystical and enigmatic qualities of tarot cards with the rich, bold flavors of dark beer. The logo combines symbolic elements from tarot iconography with traditional brewing motifs, creating a unique identity that stands out in the craft beer market. For the posters and packaging, I used a palette of deep, moody colors and intricate illustrations reminiscent of tarot artwork, aiming to evoke a sense of mystery and intrigue that invites consumers to explore the depths of Tarot Brews' flavors. The design project not only encapsulates the brewery's essence but also aims to attract and captivate a clientele that appreciates the fusion of tradition and mysticism with high-quality beer.

Hot Cow Sauce

This portfolio piece showcases my creative direction and design work for Hot Cow Chili Sauce, an innovative brand launching a new mushroom beef chili sauce. My role encompassed developing the brand identity, which included designing the logo, promotional posters, and the sauce packaging itself. The challenge was to visually communicate the unique fusion of flavors - the earthiness of mushrooms combined with the robust taste of beef and the spicy kick of chili.
